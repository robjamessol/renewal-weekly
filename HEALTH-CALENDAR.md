## Usage Notes

1. **Priority**: When multiple events overlap, pick the most widely recognized (e.g., World AIDS Day over National Handwashing Awareness Week)
2. **Relevance**: Prefer events with connections to regenerative medicine, stem cells, or conditions treatable with cell therapies
3. **Timeliness**: Only mention events within the applicable timeframe (upcoming within ~7 days or currently active)
4. **Links**: When referencing a health event, link to the specific news article or official source using `{{LINK:text|url}}` syntax
